# Cover Letter Template

Use this template structure when generating cover letters. The AI will adapt this format while personalizing the content.

---

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
[Your Phone] 

[website:https://ajolex.github.io/]
[Date]

["Dear Hiring Manager"]
[Organization Name]
[Address if known]

Re: [Position Title]

## Opening Paragraph

- Hook with specific role and organization mention
- Brief statement of enthusiasm and fit
- One sentence on how you learned about the position

## Body Paragraph 1: Relevant Experience

- Most relevant experience/accomplishment
- Specific metrics or outcomes where possible
- Direct connection to job requirements

## Body Paragraph 2: Skills & Research Alignment

- Technical skills relevant to the role
- Research interests that align with organization's mission
- Methodological expertise (impact evaluation, econometrics, etc.)

## Body Paragraph 3: Why This Organization

- Demonstrate knowledge of their work
- Explain genuine interest in their mission
- Connect your goals to their objectives

## Closing Paragraph

- Reiterate enthusiasm
- Mention availability for interview
- Thank them for consideration
- Professional sign-off

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

---

## Tips for AI Generation:

- Never use dashes (---)
- Never use words like hone, delve
- Be specific, not generic
- Quantify achievements where possible
- Show personality while remaining professional
- Keep to 300-400 words
- Avoid clich√©s like "I am writing to apply..."
- Focus on what you can contribute, not just what you want
